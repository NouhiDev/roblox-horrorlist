Roblox Horrorlist - Rating Overview

How do we rate the games?


Criteria Definition
Scariness (19%) The game's ability to invoke fear and terror in players through its atmosphere, encounters, and horror elements.
Sound Design (12%) The quality and effectiveness of the game's audio elements, including music, sound effects, and voice acting, in creating an immersive and chilling experience.
Story (10%) The narrative and plot of the game, including its depth, coherence, and ability to engage.
Visuals (10%) The graphical presentation and quality of the game, including models, environments, and visual effects.
Ambience (12%) The overall mood and environment created by the game, including lighting, level design, and other atmospheric factors.
Gameplay (19%) The mechanics, controls, and overall gameplay experience offered by the game.
Creativity (7%) The originality and innovation of the game's concepts, mechanics, and design within the horror genre.
Enjoyment (4%) The level of entertainment experienced while playing the game.
Production Quality (4%) The overall craftsmanship and polish of the game.
Technical (3%) The technical aspects of the game, such as loading times, stability, and performance.
