Roblox Horrorlist - Rating Overview

How do we rate the games?


Criteria Definition
Scariness The game's ability to invoke fear and terror in players through its atmosphere, encounters, and horror elements.
Sound Design The quality and effectiveness of the game's audio elements, including music, sound effects, and voice acting, in creating an immersive and chilling experience.
Story The narrative and plot of the game, including its depth, coherence, and ability to engage.
Visuals The graphical presentation and quality of the game, including models, environments, and visual effects.
Ambience The overall mood and environment created by the game, including lighting, level design, and other atmospheric factors.
Gameplay The mechanics, controls, and overall gameplay experience offered by the game.
Creativity The originality and innovation of the game's concepts, mechanics, and design within the horror genre.
Enjoyment The level of entertainment experienced while playing the game.
Production Quality The overall craftsmanship and polish of the game.
Technical The technical aspects of the game, such as loading times, stability, and performance.
